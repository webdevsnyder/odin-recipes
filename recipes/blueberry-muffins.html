Blueberry Muffins

Description

Blueberry Muffins with a crusty sugar topping. Quick and easy, made with few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 & 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 pint fresh blueberries - rinsed, drained and patted dry
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Position rack in the middle of oven.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  3. Spray the top of a muffin pan with non-stick coating and line with paper liners
  4. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, 1 & 1/4 cups sugar and salt until light and fluffy
  5. Beat in the eggs one at a time
  6. Mix together 1 & 3/4 cup of the flour and baking powder
  7. Beat in the flour alternately with the buttermilk, mixing just until incorporated
  8. Crush 1/4 of the blueberries and stir into the batter
  9. Mix the rest of the whole blueberries with the remaining 1/4 cup of flour and fold into the batter
  10. Scoop into muffin cups and sprinkle tops lightly with sugar
  11. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes or until golden brown and tops spring back when lightly tapped
